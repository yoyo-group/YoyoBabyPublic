# Privacy Policy for BabyLogger

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2025

## Introduction

BabyLogger ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users and their children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the BabyLogger mobile application (the "App").

**IMPORTANT:** BabyLogger is designed for parents and caregivers to track their baby's activities. We recognize that this involves collecting information about children under 13 years of age and are committed to compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## Information We Collect

### Baby Activity Data
When you use BabyLogger, you provide information about your baby's daily activities, including:

- **Sleep Information:** Sleep start/end times, duration, quality ratings, and notes
- **Feeding Information:** Feeding times, types (breast/formula), amounts, duration, and notes
- **Diaper Changes:** Change times, types (wet/dirty), rash indicators, and notes
- **Medicine Records:** Medication name, dosage, administration times, and notes
- **Baby Profile:** Baby's name, birth date, and profile photo (optional)

### Device and Usage Information
We automatically collect certain technical information:

- Device type and operating system version (iOS)
- App version and usage statistics (locally stored only)
- Error logs and crash reports (if you opt-in to share with Apple)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information
- We do NOT use tracking cookies or analytics services
- We do NOT collect location data
- We do NOT require account creation or login
- We do NOT collect information for advertising purposes

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

1. **Providing App Functionality:** Display your baby's activity data in calendar views, charts, and summaries
2. **Data Synchronization:** Sync your data across your devices using Apple's CloudKit (if enabled)
3. **Local Storage:** Store your data securely on your device using Core Data
4. **App Improvement:** Identify and fix technical issues (only if you opt-in to share crash reports with Apple)

**We do NOT:**
- Sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising or marketing
- Share data with data brokers or analytics companies
- Use your baby's information for any purpose other than providing app functionality

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All data is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's Core Data framework
- Data is protected by your device's passcode/biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)
- Data is encrypted at rest using iOS system-level encryption

### Cloud Sync (Optional)
- If you enable iCloud sync, data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit
- Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
- Data is encrypted at rest in iCloud using Apple's encryption standards
- Only you can access your iCloud data; we cannot access your iCloud storage
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings

### Data Retention
- Data is retained indefinitely until you delete it
- When you delete an activity or baby profile, it is permanently removed from your device and iCloud
- No data is retained on external servers operated by us

## COPPA Compliance

BabyLogger is designed for use by parents and caregivers (adults 18+) to track information about their babies and young children. We recognize that this involves collecting information about children under 13 years of age.

### Parental Control
- Only parents/caregivers should use BabyLogger
- Parents have complete control over their baby's data
- Parents can view, edit, and delete all data at any time
- No child-directed features or communication capabilities exist in the app

### Data Minimization
- We only collect information necessary for the app's core tracking functionality
- We do not collect persistent identifiers for children
- We do not enable children to make their information publicly available

### Third-Party Services
- We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services
- The only third-party service is Apple's CloudKit (optional), which is covered by Apple's privacy policy

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access
- You can view all your baby's data within the app at any time
- You can export your data (future feature planned)

### Correction
- You can edit any activity record or baby profile information at any time

### Deletion
- You can delete individual activities by tapping "Delete" in the activity details
- You can delete entire baby profiles and all associated data
- Deletion is immediate and permanent on your device and iCloud

### Data Portability
- Currently, data export is not available but is planned for a future update
- When available, you will be able to export all data in standard formats (CSV, JSON)

### Opt-Out of iCloud Sync
- You can disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → BabyLogger
- Disabling iCloud sync removes data from iCloud but keeps local data on your device

## Third-Party Services

BabyLogger uses the following Apple services:

### Apple CloudKit (Optional)
- Used for syncing data across your devices
- Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- Data stored in your personal iCloud account
- We do not have access to your iCloud data

## Children's Privacy

BabyLogger is NOT intended for use by children. The app is designed exclusively for parents and caregivers aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13 without proper parental consent, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

## Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your information, we will:

- Notify you promptly via in-app notification or email (if we have it)
- Provide details about what information was affected
- Explain steps we are taking to address the breach
- Advise you on steps you can take to protect yourself

## International Users

BabyLogger is currently designed for users in the United States. If you use BabyLogger from outside the United States:

- Your data is stored on your local device and in your personal iCloud account (Apple's data centers)
- Apple's CloudKit data location depends on your Apple ID country/region
- You have the same privacy rights described in this policy

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know:** You can request details about the data we collect about you
- **Right to Delete:** You can request deletion of your data (already available in-app)
- **Right to Opt-Out:** We do not sell personal information, so no opt-out is necessary
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

## GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

- Right of access, correction, and deletion (available in-app)
- Right to data portability (planned for future update)
- Right to object to processing (you can stop using the app at any time)
- Right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority
